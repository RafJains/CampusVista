# CampusVista Privacy Policy

CampusVista is designed to work offline on the user's Android device.

## Data Collection

CampusVista does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

## Camera And Photos

The app may use the camera or selected gallery images for on-device location recognition. Photos are processed locally on the device and are not uploaded to a server by the release app.

## Location

CampusVista does not collect GPS location data. The user's selected campus start point is stored locally on the device for navigation inside the app.

## Network

The release app does not require internet access for core functionality.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the CampusVista project owner.
